# About Cybersecurity Hub

## Hey, I'm Nate 👋

I'm a freshman Computer Science student at Michigan Tech University with
a cybersecurity minor. I grew up in a tech-oriented family and it was
only a matter of time before cybersecurity pulled me in.

I created Cybersecurity Hub because when I started learning cybersecurity
I found a lot of confusing, outdated, or overly technical content that
assumed you already knew everything. I wanted to build the resource I
wish I had when I started.

## My Background

I'm currently studying Computer Science at Michigan Tech, one of the top
technical universities in the country. Alongside my degree I'm pursuing
a cybersecurity minor and actively practicing hands-on security skills
through:

- **TryHackMe** — working through learning paths and rooms
- **Class labs** — applying security concepts from my coursework
- **Personal projects** — building and breaking things in my own lab

I'm also the **Chief Editor of The Byte**, the ASCII Club newspaper at
Michigan Tech — so writing and communicating technical concepts clearly
is something I take seriously.

## Why Trust This Site?

I'm not a guru claiming to have all the answers. I'm a student actively
learning cybersecurity the same way you probably are — through free
resources, labs, and a lot of trial and error.

What I can offer is honest, up-to-date information from someone who is
actually in the trenches right now. When I recommend a platform or tool
it's because I've genuinely used it, not because it pays the best
commission.

## Where I'm Headed

My goal is to become both a SOC Analyst and eventually a Penetration
Tester. I want to understand security from both sides — defensive and
offensive — and I'm documenting everything I learn along the way.

## What You'll Find Here

Every article on this site is written for people who are where I was
not long ago — curious about cybersecurity but not sure where to start.
You'll find:

- **Honest platform comparisons** — TryHackMe vs HackTheBox and more
- **Free study guides** — Security+ and beyond without expensive courses
- **Lab setup guides** — how to practice safely for free
- **Career guidance** — SOC Analyst vs Pentester and how to get hired
- **Tool tutorials** — Nmap, Burp Suite, Linux, and more

## A Note on Affiliate Links

Some links on this site are affiliate links. If you click them and make
a purchase I earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. I only
recommend tools and platforms I've actually used or thoroughly researched.
Affiliate income helps me keep this site running and free for everyone.
